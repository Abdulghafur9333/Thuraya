About Thuraya

Thuraya is more than a jewelry store—it is a tribute to a legacy. Named in honor of my late mother, Thuraya embodies elegance, strength, and timeless beauty. Each piece in our collection tells a story, preserving history in its finest form.

Our journey began with a passion for antique craftsmanship, curating rare and exquisite jewelry that transcends generations. From delicate heirlooms to bold statement pieces, Thuraya brings the past into the present, celebrating the artistry of time.
